How to Use WardConnect

  1. Select a Service: From the home page, choose the utility service (Electricity, Water, or Gas) you wish to pay for.
  2. Enter Details: Fill in the required details such as your Consumer Number and registered Phone Number.
  3. Fetch Your Bill: Click the "Fetch Bill" or "Fetch Details" button to retrieve your current dues.
  4. Make Payment: Review the details and proceed to payment. Choose your preferred payment method (UPI or Card) and complete the transaction.
  5. Download Receipt: After a successful payment, you can download a PDF receipt for your records.

Troubleshooting

  • Bill Not Found: Double-check that your Consumer Number and Phone Number are correct. Ensure there are no typos.
  • Payment Failed: If a payment fails, please check your bank account or card details and try again. Do not refresh the page during payment processing.
  • Receipt Not Downloading: Ensure you have a PDF viewer installed. Try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser.
